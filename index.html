You Are My Poetry

You are each word written, defined by emotion
Definiton of my feelings, this is my expression
The lines that fullfill the words that can't be spilled
the heart that longs for you alone

The lips have words spoken, given life through voice
yet the mind was now darken, blocked by all the noise
the whispers in ear and every words that we feel
sting like a stake straight to the bone

Let me love you dear even through the struggle
Will you love me dear even when love is trouble
the lips, the eyes, the ear have wondered
even the start know what we pondered

Why love is tough and has left us crumbled
I have drowned in its depth and is left me humbled
Yet Heaven won't fail us, Yes, it is subtle
Dearly I Love You more than I can utter
